Menu

Mandazi

Mandazi is one of the ultimate Kenyan comfort foods.For most people ,their fondest memories consist of waking up to the aroma on the weekends.There is nothing better to make you forget how bad your week has been.They hit all the right spots and goes with pretty much any drink.

Spanishfingers

Spanish fingers donuts of deep fried choux pastry coated in a cinnamon-sugar mixture will leave you licking your fingers wanting more,especially with our moreish chocolate dipping sauce.

Truffles

Sometimes we don't want to share a cake between 3-4 people,so try our delicious ,handmade truffles that are ideal for sharing and ending your meal on sweat note

Nyama Choma

One can never go wrong with nyamachoma.Nyamachoma is roasted animal meat e.g.goat meat,beef,chicken and fish.It can be eaten with kachumbari or accompany any dish you want.There's nothing that livens peoples moods faster than tanatalizing nyama choma

Mutura

Mutura is a people's favourite everyday during evening time.Mutura is goat and cow intestine which is boiled until it is almost cooked and then thrown on the grill to achieve that roasted taste .It is served with kachumbari when hot
